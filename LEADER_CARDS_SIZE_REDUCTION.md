# Leader Cards Size Reduction - Summary

## Changes Made

### Card Dimensions
- **Grid columns**: Reduced from `minmax(280px, 1fr)` to `minmax(240px, 1fr)`
- **Card padding**: Reduced from `30px 25px` to `20px 18px`
- **Card gap**: Reduced from `30px` to `20px`
- **Border radius**: Reduced from `15px` to `12px`
- **Shadow**: Lighter shadow (`0 3px 15px` instead of `0 5px 20px`)

### Typography
- **Flag emoji**: Reduced from `2.5rem` to `2rem`
- **Leader name**: Reduced from `1.25rem` to `1.05rem`
- **Leader title**: Reduced from `0.85rem` to `0.75rem`
- **Description**: Reduced from `0.95rem` to `0.85rem`

### Spacing
- **Flag margin**: Reduced from `15px` to `12px`
- **Name margin**: Reduced from `8px` to `6px`
- **Title margin**: Reduced from `12px` to `10px`
- **Line height**: Tightened from `1.6` to `1.5` for descriptions

### Section Styling
- **Background**: Changed from gradient to solid color `#f0f4f8`
- **Section padding**: Reduced from `80px 0` to `60px 0`
- **Header margin**: Reduced from `60px` to `40px`

### Responsive Updates
- **Tablet (992px)**: Grid columns `minmax(220px, 1fr)`, gap `18px`, padding `18px 16px`
- **Mobile (768px)**: Single column, gap `16px`, padding `20px`

## Result
The leader cards are now more compact and efficient, displaying more cards per row while maintaining readability and visual appeal. The solid background color provides better contrast and a cleaner look.
