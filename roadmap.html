Our Milestones

Our Strategic Roadmap

Tracking our progress from inception to national transformation. Each step brings us closer to a more united and empowered Nigeria.

2022 - Q1

Foundation & Launch

Official establishment of the United Youth Nigeria Movement. Initial meetings with youth leaders across 6 geo-political zones to define our core mission.

2023 - Q2

Pilot Empowerment Programs

Launched digital literacy and leadership bootcamps in Lagos, Abuja, and Kano. Over 1,000 youth certified in various skill sets.

2024 - Q3

National Expansion

Extended our footprint to 24 states. Established university chapters and community impact hubs to drive local innovation and civic engagement.

2025 - Q4

Policy & Advocacy Focus

Active participation in national youth policy dialogues. Collaboration with federal agencies to integrate youth-led solutions into government programs.

2026 - Looking Ahead

Unified Youth Portal

Launching a nationwide digital platform to connect millions of Nigerian youth to global opportunities, mentorship, and funding.
