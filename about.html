About UYNM

The United Youth Nigeria Movement (UYNM) is a transformative platform focused on empowering young Nigerians with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to drive positive change. We are a community-led movement dedicated to fostering leadership, innovation, and sustainable development across the 36 states of the federation and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT).

Our Commitment

UYNM is dedicated to creating a generation of leaders who are not afraid to take initiative and contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's growth and development.

Join the Movement

We believe that by coming together with common voice, we can achieve more. Join us as we work towards a brighter future for our nation and empower the next generation.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and empower young Nigerians who are committed to making a positive impact on their communities and the country at large. We will provide a platform for youth to engage, collaborate, and develop the skills and networks necessary to drive change.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where young people are empowered to lead, innovate, and drive sustainable development. A country where meritocracy, inclusivity, and justice are the hallmarks of governance and social progress for all citizens.

Our Core
Values

Inclusivity : Embracing Nigerian diversity and promoting unity
Meritocracy : Leadership based on competence and integrity
Innovation : Encouraging creativity and problem-solving
Accountability : Promoting transparency and good governance
Global citizenship : Preparing youths for global engagement

In-Depth Analysis

Leadership Styles & Approaches

Detailed analysis of leadership styles from both civilian and military leaders, offering youth the opportunity to study diverse approaches to governance and national development.

NG

Chief Olusegun Obasanjo

Former President of Nigeria (1999-2007)

Leadership Style

A mix of authoritarianism and populism, characterized by decisive and effective decision-making with emphasis on national unity and stability.

Key Achievements

Implemented significant economic reforms including the Structural Adjustment Program (SAP) and National Economic Empowerment and Development Strategy (NEEDS). Stabilized Nigeria's economy, promoted democracy, and advanced Pan-Africanism.

Leadership Approach

Reflects military background with strong focus on accountability and national development. Known for decisive action but also criticized for authoritarian tendencies.

NG

Gen Buba Marwa

Chairman, National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA)

Leadership Style

Marked by a focus on accountability and reform. Collaborative and inclusive approach, seeking to engage stakeholders and promote collective responsibility.

Key Achievements

Transformed NDLEA operations with emphasis on leadership accountability in addressing Nigeria's drug challenges. Acknowledged impact of reforms on various groups while supporting affected communities.

Leadership Approach

Reflects extensive public service experience and commitment to good governance. Emphasizes cultivating leadership qualities early in life and addressing root causes of national challenges.

NG

Sen George Akume

Minister of Special Duties & Inter-Governmental Affairs

Leadership Style

Characterized by neutrality and an approachable, collaborative style. Balances firmness with accessibility in governance.

Key Achievements

Served in various roles including Governor and Minister, demonstrating versatility in public service. Known for fostering inter-governmental cooperation and national unity.

Leadership Approach

Shaped by extensive public service experience and unwavering commitment to national development. Emphasizes dialogue, consensus-building, and inclusive governance.

Our youth have the opportunity to study the leadership styles of Nigerian leaders from both civilian and military backgrounds, learning from their diverse approaches, achievements, and challenges in nation-building.
