Who We Are

About Us

The United Youth Nigeria Movement (UYNM) is a transformative platform focused on empowering young Nigerians with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to drive positive change. It is a call to action for talented, passionate, and visionary youth to take the lead in solving the nation's most pressing challenges. Our work is bringing everyone on board to ensure no one is left behind as we strive to create a common goal, building a better Nigeria for all.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and empower young Nigerians who are committed to making a positive impact on their communities and the country at large. We will provide a platform for youth to engage, collaborate, and develop the skills, knowledge, and networks necessary to drive change.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where young people are empowered to lead, innovate, and drive sustainable development. A country where meritocracy, inclusivity, and justice are the hallmarks of governance. A nation that is respected globally for its integrity, innovation, and contribution to humanity.

Our Core
Values

  • Inclusivity: Embracing all young people irrespective of background context.
  • Meritocracy: Rewarding excellence, competence, and integrity.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking and problem-solving.
  • Accountability: Promoting transparent and good governance.
  • Unity: Fostering collaboration towards a common goal.
  • Patriotism: Inspiring love and loyalty for Nigeria.

Who can join us

  • Visionary young leaders
  • Innovative thinkers and entrepreneurs
  • Community development advocates
  • Students and researchers
  • Change-makers ready to challenge the status quo
