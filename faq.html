Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the United Youth Nigeria Movement

The United Youth Nigeria Movement (UYNM) is a transformative platform focused on empowering young Nigerians with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to drive positive change. We bring together talented, passionate, and visionary youth to take the lead in solving the nation's most pressing challenges.

UYNM is open to young Nigerians who are passionate about creating positive change in their communities and the nation at large. We welcome youth from all backgrounds who demonstrate leadership potential and commitment to our vision.

Our programs are primarily designed for young Nigerians aged 18-35 years. However, we also have specific initiatives for younger participants and mentorship opportunities for those who exceed the age limit but wish to contribute to our mission.

You can apply to join UYNM by clicking the "Become a Member" button on our website and filling out the membership application form. The process is simple and requires basic information about yourself and your interests.

No, there is no application fee to join UYNM. Our membership is free as we believe in making opportunities accessible to all young Nigerians regardless of their financial background.

Members gain access to leadership training programs, networking opportunities, mentorship from experienced professionals, career development resources, and the chance to participate in impactful community projects. You'll also be part of a vibrant community of like-minded young leaders.

We offer a hybrid approach with both online and physical programs. Many of our training sessions and workshops are available online to ensure accessibility, while we also organize in-person events, conferences, and community projects across Nigeria.

While UYNM doesn't provide direct funding to individuals, we connect members with funding opportunities, grants, and partnerships. We also support project ideas through our incubation programs and help members access resources needed to bring their initiatives to life.

The application process typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete. Once submitted, our team reviews applications within 5-7 business days, and you'll receive a confirmation email with further steps.

Yes! We welcome volunteers for specific events and projects. However, becoming a member gives you access to more opportunities and allows you to be more deeply involved in our mission.

Membership is ongoing and doesn't require annual renewal. However, we encourage active participation to maintain your membership status and continue receiving benefits.

We offer leadership development programs, skills training workshops, community outreach initiatives, advocacy campaigns, entrepreneurship support, and mentorship programs. Our programs cover areas like governance, technology, agriculture, health, and education.

We run programs throughout the year with varying frequencies. Some programs are monthly, others are quarterly, and we also have annual flagship events. Check our events calendar for the latest schedule.

Absolutely! We encourage members to propose innovative programs and projects that align with our mission. Submit your proposal through our member portal, and our team will review it for potential implementation.

For membership application, you typically need a valid ID, a recent passport photograph, and basic contact information. Specific programs may require additional documents like CVs or letters of recommendation.

Yes, you can apply for multiple programs simultaneously as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each program. We encourage members to explore various opportunities that align with their interests and goals.

If your application is not successful, you'll receive feedback on areas for improvement. You're welcome to reapply for future cohorts or explore other programs within UYNM that may be better suited to your current profile.

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Contact us at partnerships@uynm.org with details about your organization and proposed collaboration areas. Our partnerships team will reach out to discuss potential opportunities.

We engage in program partnerships, funding partnerships, knowledge-sharing collaborations, advocacy partnerships, and corporate social responsibility initiatives. Each partnership is tailored to create maximum impact for Nigerian youth.

Yes, we actively collaborate with international organizations, foundations, and development agencies committed to youth empowerment in Nigeria. We value diverse partnerships that bring global best practices and resources to our programs.
